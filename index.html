The MorseGrid method

Complete, end-to-end engagements.

We don't drop a tool and walk away. Every engagement ends with documentation, a recorded walkthrough, and the keys so the system works without us.

1

Diagnose your operations

Every engagement begins with a conversation. We listen first: how the business actually operates, where the energy is leaking, and what the team has already tried. From there, we map one specific workflow we'd improve and show you what that work would look like.

Build the system

If it's a fit, we build over the next two to four weeks. We share progress as we go. You see the work every week. By the time we hand it over, there are no surprises. If something's off, we fix it before you pay the final invoice.

About

I'm Maksudur Rahman.
People call me Maks.

I started doing this work because I spent years inside small businesses watching smart people lose hours every week to things a quiet system could have handled. The owners knew it. They couldn't afford to hire someone full-time to fix it, and the agencies they talked to wanted to sell them something larger and shinier than what they actually needed.

So I started a small studio that does only this: looks at how a business actually runs, identifies what shouldn't require a human, and builds the thing that quietly takes it off their plate.

I work with a few specialists I trust: a ClickUp consultant, a couple of engineers, a designer. We take on a small number of clients each year. We diagnose first. We price after we understand the problem. And what we build belongs to you when we leave.

If you'd like to talk, the door is below.

Things people ask

Common questions.

"How is this different from hiring a freelancer?"

Mostly: we document everything, we build for handoff, and we're around afterward if you want us to be. A freelancer is a pair of hands. We're closer to a small studio.

"What if my industry isn't on your list?"

The patterns transfer. We've worked across e-commerce, health, construction, consulting, and education. If you'd like, write to us and we'll tell you honestly whether it's a fit.

"What if I don't like what you build?"

You see the work in progress, every week. By the time we hand it over, there should be no surprises. If something's off, we fix it before you pay the final invoice.

"Where are you based?"

Maks is in Dhaka. The team works asynchronously with clients in Australia, the United States, and Belgium. We're moving to Dublin in autumn 2026.
