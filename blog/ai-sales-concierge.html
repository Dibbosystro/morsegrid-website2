The Anatomy of an AI Sales Concierge: From Booking to Proposal in 2 Minutes

🤖
MorseGrid Team
Automation Specialists

What if a new lead could go from filling out your contact form to receiving a personalized, fully-formatted proposal - all before your team even sits down for their morning coffee? That's not science fiction. It's a workflow we've deployed for multiple B2B clients, and in this post we're going to show you exactly how it works.

The Problem with Traditional Sales Processes

The average B2B sales cycle takes far longer than it needs to. A lead fills out a form, waits 24 hours for a response, gets on a discovery call, waits another few days for a proposal, and by the time they receive it, they've already talked to two competitors. Speed is the ultimate competitive advantage in B2B sales.

The Architecture of an AI Sales Concierge

A fully automated AI sales concierge is made up of several interconnected modules. Here's how each layer works:

Layer 1: Intelligent Lead Capture

When a lead submits a form or books a call via Cal.com, a Make.com scenario fires instantly. It enriches the lead's data using Apollo.io or Clearbit - pulling their company size, industry, revenue, tech stack, and LinkedIn URL - and creates a fully populated deal in your CRM automatically.

Layer 2: AI Company Research

Before the first human ever looks at the lead, an OpenAI GPT-4 call runs a full company research brief. It summarizes what the company does, identifies their likely pain points, pulls recent news, and generates talking points tailored to your service offering. This brief lands in the CRM deal notes and in the rep's Slack DM - all before they pick up the phone.

Layer 3: Automated Proposal Generation

After the discovery call (transcribed and summarised automatically by AI), another workflow triggers. It takes the call summary, the company brief, and your service pricing - and uses GPT-4 to draft a fully personalised proposal. That draft is pushed into PandaDoc or HubSpot Quotes, formatted with your branding, and sent to the prospect for e-signature.

Layer 4: Follow-up & Nurture Sequences

If the proposal isn't signed within 48 hours, an automated follow-up sequence kicks in. Personalised check-in emails, LinkedIn connection requests, and even WhatsApp messages (for warm leads) are sent at optimised intervals. Every touchpoint is logged back to the CRM automatically.

The Results

Clients who have deployed this system report a 40% reduction in time-to-proposal, a 25% increase in proposal acceptance rates (because proposals are better-researched and faster), and reps reclaiming 8–10 hours per week previously spent on admin.

"Our reps were spending half their day putting data into the CRM. Now the CRM does the work and they just sell. It's been a complete shift in how we operate."
- Head of Sales, B2B SaaS client

What You Need to Build This

  • Make.com or n8n - for workflow orchestration
  • OpenAI GPT-4 API - for research and proposal drafting
  • Apollo.io or Clearbit - for lead enrichment
  • PandaDoc or HubSpot Quotes - for proposal generation
  • Cal.com - for booking and scheduling triggers
